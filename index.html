Why we're signing

Every transfer window brings the same promise — ambition, evolution, a squad built to compete on every front. Supporters are asking for it to actually show up on the team sheet this season, not just in the press conference.

This isn't about any one name. It's about backing the manager with the right profile of player: proven at the level we want to compete at, hungry, and able to start from day one.

  • 01 Recruit for immediate impact Signings ready to start now, not projects for 2028.
  • 02 Fill the positions that actually need it Listen to the manager's stated priorities, not the market's leftovers.
  • 03 Match spending to ambition If the target is the top four, the business has to reflect that.
  • 04 Move early, not on deadline day Give new signings a preseason, not a introduction mid-crisis.
